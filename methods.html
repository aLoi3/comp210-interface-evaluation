Heuristic Evaluation - Ultimate expert Ryan Clarke, Prime expert Adrian Carter and ... will take part in evaluating the game.

Think aloud (needs a camera behind the expert) - In addition to heuristic evaluation, I will be using think aloud method, which is considered as one of the sub-methods to psycho-physiological method. It requires a camera behind an expert that records whatever the expert says. And the expert has to include his/her impressions about the game, how he/she feels while playing it, what events made him/her feel most emotional? This method should not take more than 10 minutes, as the game itself is not completely finished and saying what on one's mind while playing a game for a long period of time ruins player experience and therefore unnecessary or dishonest feedback will be given.
